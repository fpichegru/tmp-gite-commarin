Welcome to La Traversée du Bonheur

The aim of our guest house is to make you discover a quiet place in a genuine family like atmosphere. In a peaceful village of Burgundy called Commarin far from noisy towns… a place where you will feel like living freely at your own rythm.

The place used to be a large cowshed which has been totally renovated(a mixture of modern and ancient style for ex. ceilings with exposed beams) . We have got a big garden too.

The surroundings are very rich with historical sites (medieval places, the Burgundy vineyard Road,…).

We will provide you with some good adresses (restaurant , burgundy wine cellars to visit, and lots of activities…).Very near we have got a lake (a nice place where to rest ) and beautitul forests you can walk through - Cycling could be also practiced along the canal.

We hope you will enjoy visiting our website and we are looking forward to making you discover in real this wonderful place.

« Far from towns, far from noisy people, a place where we take time to live , to smile, to breathe »
