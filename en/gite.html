The House

A country Guest House for 6 persons

Charm, modern Confort & Conviviality - 3 Bedrooms

Welcome to « La Traversée du Bonheur » situated right in the middle of the french region « Bourgogne Franche-Comté » , in a lovely little village, for a long or short stay according to your wishes.

The guest house is facing the Château of Commarin . A few minutes of the Panthier Lake , of medevial sites, in less than one hour’s drive you’ll get to Dijon ( the main city), Beaune and the vineyards of Burgundy. We are very near Morvan which is a very picturesque area.

Terrace & Outside

200 meters above the house you can enjoy a magnificent sight on a beautiful lanscape. A pique-nique area is available there.

As for the house itself you will enjoy a spacy garden(600m²) , a nice resting place especially for summer evenings .

Living / Dining Room

The house itself comprehends a large living space (with exposed beams) creating a nice and cosy atmosphere (dining room , sitting room, kitchen area with its open bar).
